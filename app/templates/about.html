About Slash


Slash is a simple tool which makes URLs as short as possible.

With Slash, you can:

  • Customize your shortened URLs.
  • Generate QR codes for your shortened URLs.
  • Track your shortened URL's performance.
  • View how many visits your shortened URL has received.
  • See the history of URLs you have created so that you can easily find and reuse them.
